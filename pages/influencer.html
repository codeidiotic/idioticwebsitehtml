How does Idiotic Media Payment Works?

Upon completion of deliverables for a campaign, you can choose to receive the payment within 10 Working days by accepting a 10% commission to be given to Idiotic Media. If you are not in a hurry to receive the payment, all payments are cleared within 10-45 days of campaign completion. All the payments are processed directly into your bank account through our secure system.

How should I price my listing on Idiotic Media?

You have complete freedom to set your prices for campaigns, you only need to make sure that your profile and content created will resonate to the price you set.
